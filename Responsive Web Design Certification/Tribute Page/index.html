The Beatles

The Beatles were an English rock band, formed in Liverpool in the 1960s

They were active for 10 years and published 13 studio albums

Here's a list of The Beatles' albums:

  • Please Please Me (1963)
  • With the Beatles (1963)
  • A Hard Day's Night (1964)
  • Beatles for Sale(1964)
  • Help! (1965)
  • Rubber Soul (1965)
  • Revolver (1966)
  • Sgt. Peppers Lonely Hearts Club Band (1967)
  • Magical Mystery Tour (1967)
  • The Beatles ("The White Album") (1968)
  • Yellow Submarine (1969)
  • Abbey Road (1969)
  • Let It Be (1970)
